About Us


The website will be targeted at people who first visit to Sarnia. In this web site, users will be able to search the locations of the nice restaurants around them, and people’s reviews on them about menu, price, interior, and atmosphere, etc. In addition to the above information, the site will tell users how to reach the restaurants that users have found out on bus maps. Also, users can post and share their text and photo reviews on the restaurants that they visited on this website. This function will make this website’s contents plentiful. Other functions may be added appropriately based on our understanding of Java’s various new technologies as this project progresses.
